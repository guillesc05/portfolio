GUILLERMO SÁNCHEZ

Videogame Developer, 2D & 3D Artist

Hi! My name is Guillermo Sánchez, a videogame developer and a multidisciplinary artist living in Spain.
I am currently studying at Complutense University of Madrid, in my third year of the Videogame Development Degree.

On the one hand, I specialize in game development, I mainly work with programming languages C++ and C#, although I am familiar with JavaScript, HTML and CSS. I have a passion for code architecture in the games I work on, and I am always looking forward to learning something new every day.

On the other hand, I specialize in 3d Art using Blender, which I am self taught in. I have a great interest in combining different feelings and styles to create an original end product. This, paired with my game development knowledge, makes me be able to tie it all in and adapt my art into the medium.

Overall, I am a programmer who loves to mix the technical and artistical side of games to deliver a unique product.
